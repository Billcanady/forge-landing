The FORGE Intake

Five questions. About fifteen minutes. The truth about where your business stands.

This is Bill Canady's diagnostic — the five things he'd ask you first on a live call: your number, where your growth really comes from, your team, where you're the bottleneck, and what you actually want. It runs one question at a time.

This isn't a sales quiz. It's built to show you where you truly are — and the depth we work at inside FORGE. Vague answers produce a useless result. Talk like you would to another operator: numbers, names, dates.

At the end you get a branded diagnostic you can keep, you can book your FORGE call, and we review every intake and reach out.

💬Tip — talk instead of type. Turn on voice-to-text and just speak your answers. Mac: the dictation key or fn fn. iPhone: the mic on the keyboard. Windows: Win + H.
