FORGE is a 12-week guided path through five disciplines, with one capstone artifact per discipline. It's self-paced inside that window — most operators install the core system in the 12 weeks and run the cadence ongoing.
Who is FORGE for?
CEOs and owners of public or private companies up to roughly $20M in revenue, and the division or business-unit leaders running a P&L inside a larger company. If you're at the point where the business has to become a real business — real processes, a real team, a real plan — you're who this is built for.

We cover investment on your fit call, once we've confirmed FORGE is right for your situation. The intake and the call come first — that's how we make sure it's a fit before anyone talks numbers.

Many of our best operators have. FORGE doesn't waste that muscle — it operates at the altitude the middle-market and growth-stage CEO actually needs, with the Rule of Three architecture on top.

FORGE installs faster with your #2 or core team in the work. Tell us your setup on the fit call and we'll point you to the right configuration.
